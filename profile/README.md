## 🌟 What is WaEnhancerX?
WaEnhancerX is a community-driven, heavily optimized Xposed/LSPosed module designed to supercharge your WhatsApp experience. Built on a standalone, robust architecture, it offers seamless integration, ultimate privacy controls, extreme visual customizations, and powerful utilities.

We believe in transparency and community: **Every single core feature you use right now will remain 100% free forever.** No existing tools will ever be put behind a paywall!

---

## 🔥 Core Features

### 🛡️ Privacy & Security
* **Anti-Revoke:** Prevent senders from deleting messages and statuses (Delete for Everyone & Delete for Me).
* **Anti-View Once:** View "View Once" media infinitely and easily save it to your gallery.
* **Ghost Mode:** Completely hide your online presence, typing status, and recording indicators.
* **Read Receipt Controls:** Hide blue ticks and second ticks (delivery reports) effortlessly.

### 🎨 Visual Customization
* **Custom Themes V2:** Complete control over chat colors, message bubbles, and toolbars.
* **IG-Style Status:** View statuses directly from the home screen just like Instagram.
* **UI Clean-up:** Hide unwanted tabs, remove channel recommendations, and separate groups from personal chats.

### 🛠️ Advanced Utilities
* **Call Recording:** Automatically record incoming and outgoing calls in high-quality AAC format.
* **Smart Automation:** Message Scheduler and Auto-Reply for offline automation *(Coming Soon)*.
* **Translation & Transcripts:** Built-in Google Translate integration and Audio Voice Notes transcription.
* **Tasker Integration:** Create advanced and complex automation flows using Tasker.

### 📁 Media & Data Management
* **Media Unlocked:** Bypass share limits and status video length restrictions.
* **High-Quality Media:** Send images and videos without native compression.
* **Quick Downloader:** One-click downloads for any status or profile picture.

## 🚀 How to Get Started
Ready to take your messaging experience to the next level?

1. **Root your device** and install the **LSPosed Framework**.
2. Download the latest `WaEnhancerX` APK from our [Official Repository](https://github.com/mubashardev/WaEnhancerX).
3. Enable the module in LSPosed and Force Stop WhatsApp.
4. Open the app and enjoy the magic! ✨

> **Pro Tip:** For the best and most stable experience, always keep auto-updates for WhatsApp disabled and only update to versions explicitly supported by WaEnhancerX.
